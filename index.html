Frequently Asked Questions

Our Stencil Maker is an AI-powered image effect that instantly transforms your photos into high-quality stencil art. It intelligently analyzes your image and converts it into a simplified, high-contrast design perfect for crafts, DIY projects, and artistic creations.

The process is incredibly fast. While the exact time depends on the complexity of your uploaded image, our AI is optimized to generate your stencil in just a few seconds, providing one of the fastest outputs available.

For optimal results, use images with clear subjects, good lighting, and strong contrast between the subject and the background. Photos with uncluttered backgrounds, such as portraits or isolated objects, tend to create the cleanest stencils.
